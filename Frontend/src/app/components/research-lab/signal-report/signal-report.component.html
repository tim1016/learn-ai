Mathematical Appendix
Sharpe Ratio (Annualized)
  • r̄ — mean bar return
  • r_f — risk-free rate (assumed 0)
  • sigma_r — standard deviation of bar returns
  • Annualized by sqrt(252 x 390) for 1-minute bars
Effective Sample Size (Neff)
  • N — raw sample size
  • rho_k — autocorrelation at lag k
  • Summation truncated (Newey-West) when rho_k < 0.05
Maximum Drawdown
  • P_t — portfolio value at time t
  • Measures peak-to-trough decline in equity curve
Parameter Stability
  • sigma_Sharpe(theta) — std of Sharpe across thresholds
  • S̄(theta) — mean Sharpe across thresholds
  • Higher is better: 1.0 = perfectly stable
Alpha Decay (OOS Sharpe Trend)
  • S_i — OOS Sharpe of fold i
  • beta_1 — slope (negative = decay)
  • t-test assesses statistical significance of the trend
Annualized Turnover
  • w_t — portfolio weight at time t
  • Measures frequency of position changes
  • Lower turnover reduces transaction costs
