How to Interpret

Strong Signal (IC >= 0.03, p < 0.05)

Indicator shows statistically significant predictive power. Consider using at this horizon.

Weak Signal (IC 0.01-0.03 or p 0.05-0.10)

Some predictive content but may not survive transaction costs. Use with caution.

Noise (IC < 0.01 or p >= 0.10)

No detectable predictive power at this horizon. Try different parameters or horizons.

Best Horizon

The horizon with highest |IC| and statistical significance. Use this for trading decisions.
