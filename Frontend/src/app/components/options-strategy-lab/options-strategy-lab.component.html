Black-Scholes Formulae Used

d₁ = [ln(S/K) + (r + σ²/2) × T] / (σ × √T)

d₂ = d₁ − σ × √T

Call Price = S × N(d₁) − K × e⁻ʳᵀ × N(d₂)

Put Price = K × e⁻ʳᵀ × N(−d₂) − S × N(−d₁)

Leg P&L = position_sign × (BS_Price − Premium_Paid) × Quantity

where position_sign = +1 for Long, −1 for Short (i.e., Long P&L = (Value − Premium) × Qty, Short P&L = (Premium − Value) × Qty)

Expiration P&L = position_sign × (Intrinsic − Premium_Paid) × Quantity

where Intrinsic = max(S − K, 0) for Calls, max(K − S, 0) for Puts

Total P&L = Σ (all leg P&Ls)

N(x) = standard normal CDF (Abramowitz & Stegun approximation, error < 7.5×10⁻⁸)
