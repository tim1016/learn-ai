What was locked in upstream

From the v2 handoffs on master. Read these before iterating on the artifacts below.

P1.4 — cap
Background poller in the launcher, os.scandir walk, _WORKSPACE_POLL_INTERVAL_S = 1.0 as a non-configurable module constant. KillReason enum threaded through _kill_container_via_cidfile. Threat model: benign overrun only — escalate to kernel-enforced (--storage-opt size=) if auth ever lands.
P2.5 — window
Half-open [09:30 ET of start_date, 09:30 ET of next_trading_day(end_date)). New trading_calendar.py as the single source of truth for validator and staging. Half-days rejected, weekends rejected, DST resolved through pandas_market_calendars. Frontend gets a /api/lean-sidecar/calendar/blocked-dates endpoint and a custom popover that disables blocked dates.
