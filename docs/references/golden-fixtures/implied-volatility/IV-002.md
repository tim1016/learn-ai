# IV-002 — SVI Total Variance Surface

**Status:** active · v1 (2026-05-08)
**Category:** options-pricing
**Canonical:** `PythonDataService/app/volatility/fitting.py::fit_svi`

## What this pins

The SVI calibration accuracy on a noiseless input. Three parameter sets × 7 log-moneyness values are constructed from the Gatheral formula; `fit_svi` must recover the oracle total variance within 1e-6.

## Oracle

Direct formula evaluation: `w(k) = a + b·(ρ·(k−m) + √((k−m)²+σ²))` — Gatheral (2004), applied without calling `fit_svi`.

## Reference

Gatheral, J. (2004). "A parsimonious arbitrage-free implied volatility parameterization with application to the valuation of volatility derivatives." Presentation at the Global Derivatives & Risk Management 2004.

## Agreement

scipy `least_squares` converges to ~1e-7 residuals on noiseless SVI input. Pinned tolerance: `atol=1e-6, rtol=0.0`.

## Input Grid

21 rows = 3 SVI parameter sets × 7 log-moneyness values:

|-----|-------|------|-------|-------|-------|
| 0 | 0.020 | 0.30 | -0.70 | 0.00 | 0.20 |
| 1 | 0.040 | 0.50 | -0.40 | 0.05 | 0.15 |
| 2 | 0.010 | 0.20 | -0.50 | -0.05 | 0.25 |

k values: [-0.30, -0.15, -0.05, 0.00, 0.05, 0.15, 0.30]; forward=100, ttm=0.5
