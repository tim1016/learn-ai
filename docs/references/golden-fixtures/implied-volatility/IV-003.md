# IV-003 — IV30 Constant-Maturity (Variance-Time Interpolation)

**Status:** active · v1 (2026-05-08)
**Category:** options-pricing
**Canonical:** `PythonDataService/app/volatility/vix_replication.py::vix_style_iv30`

## What this pins

The full IV30 calculation pipeline: CBOE per-expiry variance integral + variance-time interpolation to 30-day constant maturity. 3 test cases with two BSM-priced 5-strike chains each (bracketing 30 days).

## Oracle

CBOE VIX 2019 whitepaper formula applied directly in Python (same algorithm, independently coded). Variance-time interpolation per Demeterfi, Derman, Kamal, Zou (1999).

## References

- CBOE VIX 2019 whitepaper "VIX Index Calculation: Step-by-Step"
- Demeterfi, Derman, Kamal, Zou (1999). "More Than You Ever Wanted to Know About Volatility Swaps." Goldman Sachs Quantitative Strategies Research Notes.

## Agreement

Oracle and canonical implement identical arithmetic. Max observed float64 discrepancy: < 1e-12. Pinned tolerance: `atol=1e-6, rtol=0.0`.

## Fixture Design

Both `call_mid` and `put_mid` are stored for all strikes so the test can provide full `OptionQuote` objects. `vix_style_iv30` uses put-call parity at K* to recover the forward — setting only one side to zero would produce a wrong forward.

## Input Grid

3 cases; each has two chains of 5 BSM-priced strikes (90%/95%/100%/105%/110% of spot):

| Case | spot | rate | σ_T1 | σ_T2 | T1 | T2 |
|------|------|------|------|------|-----|-----|
| 0 | 100 | 0.05 | 0.25 | 0.27 | 25d | 35d |
| 1 | 100 | 0.02 | 0.20 | 0.22 | 20d | 40d |
| 2 | 100 | 0.04 | 0.30 | 0.28 | 28d | 32d |
